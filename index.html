Projects

Below a deep dive into the projects I'm most proud of.

UEs in VR

UEs in VR explores the possibility of utilizing and improving the User Enactments technique by emulating it in virtual reality.
A smart home environment with realistic physics and interactions has been developed in order to explore the user perception of a (simulated) pro-active AI system present in the house.

Divergences

The Divergences project is an artistic work started by the visual artist Federica Sasso and sound designer Luca Pagan, with the collaboration of the Rehab Technology Lab of Italian Institute of Technology.
The test user with a prosthetic leg is immersed in an infinite corridor virtual environment with four different lightings and surfaces (marble, water, moquette and grass. While walking on a tapis roulant the virtual environment accordingly. For each setup the user experiences different footsteps sound triggered by devices able to detect pressure. The virtual environment, empowered by the sound system, aims to explore the connection between the body, sound impulses and the digital world.
An exhibition of the project occured at Ars Electronica Festival 2023.

Embodiment

The embodiment project, started by Federica Sasso, is a way to raise awareness on post-pandemic social difficulties. The user is familiarized with the new virtual body, while the Liminal Space virtual environment fills with a crowd of people that gradually surrounds the user.
Highlights of the project have been exhibited at the PhotoVogue Festival.

BoloFence

BoloFence is a concept of an Android turistic app of the Bologna city. The app recognizes the user mobility type (walk, bike or car) and accordingly triggers a notification when the user enters an area of interest. A dashboard collects the user path data, preserving the privacy, and provides a clustering tool able to detect new potential areas of interest.
