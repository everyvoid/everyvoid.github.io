Divergences

The Divergences project is an artistic work started by the visual artist Federica Sasso and sound deisgner Luca Pagan, with the collaboration of the Rehab Technology Lab of Italian Institute of Technology.
It consists in immersing the test user with a prosthetic leg in an infinite corridor environment with four different setups. For each setup the user experiences different footsteps sound triggered by devices able to detect pressure. The virtual environment, empowered by the sound system, aims to explore the connection between the body, sound impulses and digital reality.
An exhibition of the project occured at Ars Electronica Festival 2023.
